Welcome to the repository for Ctrl+Alt+She 

Team Lead: Heather Simpson-Scott

Our Solution:

We have developed a smart desk booking app designed to improve how we book desks within the bank.
Before this solution, teams had limited visibility of desk preferences and inefficient coordination when trying to reserve areas for team collaboration and seating together. 
Our app streamlines the booking process through real-time availability and profile-based preferences, helping us find and reserve desks that suit our individual and team needs.
